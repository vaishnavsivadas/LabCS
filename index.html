About The Platform

This initiative aims at providing practical training for trainees taking up courses related to Cyber Security at ITS, INS Valsura. The content is structured in such a way that after completion of each training module, the practical assessment can be completed here. Moreover we also intend to give exposure to CTF (Capture The Flag) challenges for the trainees by integrating support to PicoCTF.
